Projects

Chest Freezer JIG


Design and Development of a 1250-liter Chest Freezer JIG capable of producing three different models (750 liters, 1000 liters, and 1250 liters capacity) by simply changing the plug and adjusting the side doors. The machine stands at 5 meters in height, 5 meters in length, and 3 meters in width.
An electrically controlled hydraulic actuator is seamlessly integrated to provide a reciprocating motion to the plug. The plug's open and close function is fully mechanical, ensuring precise and reliable operation.
These convertible large chest freezers boast high capacity, reaching -30 degrees Celsius within a remarkable 40-minute timeframe. This design offers flexibility, efficiency, and rapid cooling capabilities for diverse freezer requirements.

Condenser


Our primary objective is to design an efficient and effective forced-type air-cooled condenser for chest freezers. The condenser must possess the following characteristics: Compact Design: It should be able to fit into every standard freezer, ensuring optimal use of space. Versatility: The condenser should have the capacity to handle visi coolers, catering to a range of freezer types. Tube Length: The length of the tube should not be less than 25 meters, utilizing 3/16" copper-coated steel tubes. Condenser Type: The condenser design should follow a zig-zag pattern, ensuring optimal heat exchange efficiency. By incorporating these features, we aim to deliver a condenser that meets high-performance standards, addresses spatial constraints, and accommodates various freezer configurations.
